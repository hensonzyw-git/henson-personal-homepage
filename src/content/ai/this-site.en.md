This site is my first AI-practice entry — it is itself the evidence of "turning ideas into something hands-on through AI collaboration".

Technically it's an Astro static site: Chinese by default with an English mirror, blog / AI-practice content managed as Markdown content collections, system fonts plus self-hosted Space Mono with no external font CDN (so it loads reliably in China too).

It was built through AI coworking: feed the PRD and design spec to the model, then generate routes, components and styles conversationally, with me making the product trade-offs and calibration. The site keeps evolving — from Astro version upgrades to correcting content against my knowledge base — all within this self-referential practice.
